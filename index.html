Texas

Texas is the second-largest state in the United States and is known for its diverse landscapes, rich history, and strong cultural identity. From major cities like Houston, Dallas, and Austin to wide-open ranchlands and Gulf Coast beaches, Texas offers a wide variety of environments and lifestyles. The state played an important role in American history, including its time as an independent republic before joining the United States in 1845. Today, Texas is recognized for its energy industry, growing technology sector, and vibrant mix of cultures that influence its food, music, and traditions.
